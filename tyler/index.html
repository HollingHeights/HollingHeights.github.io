Fish

Pike

Pike have huge teeth. They can break a fishing line easily with its teeth. Found in fresh waters. They are verocious and are carnivores but can be eaten. They eat a lot of things though too.

Catfish

There are a lot of varieties of catfish. Catfish don't usually have big teeth. They are carnivores but can still be eaten.

Paddlefish

Paddlefish are kind of rare. They are see through sometimes when they are little. They are illegal to eat. They get pretty big too. They live in North America and China.
